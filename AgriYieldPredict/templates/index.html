Introduction

India's economy primarily depends on agriculture yield growth and their allied agro industry products. There are many types of problem with our farmers. Now a days the farmers in India farming in traditional way also. They are not upgrading themselves. They farm some crops which he had farmed in many past years, they don’t know that there will be profit or loss and they get loss many times.

So to reduce this type of things we are going build a project “Agriculture Yield Forecasting”, where we forecast the list of beneficial crops for the particular yield in particular month or season.

By this we can forecast the profitable crop for particular yield and if farmer farms this crop then that may be a better option to gain the profit.
